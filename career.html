Career Opportunities

Every intervention is tailored for each person. Our clinical team ensures that the procedures and interventions used, meet the needs of the person. We are all different, so this holds true for every person we serve. Our home office is located in the San Fernando Valley, and we are currently expanding to Orange County with the opening of our satellite office. We provide services in various locations, such as, Los Angeles County, Antelope Valley, Santa Ana, West Minister, New Port Beach, Long Beach, Pasadena, Anaheim, San Gabriel, San Fernando Valley, etc. We are looking to hire full/part time Behavior Technicians (BT), BCaBA's, and BCBA's. BCBA's will be considered for a Clinical Director position at our Orange County office. Please take a moment to review the qualifications and duties listed below.

Behavior Technician (BT)

Qualifications

  • Member of the clinical team providing direct services in the home under the supervision and guidance of the case specialist, supervisor, and clinical team
  • Provides 1:1 behavioral services to children with autism and related disorders in the home and clinic
  • Attends scheduled team meetings, related observations, and demonstrates behavior interventions and programs
  • Implements behavior intervention strategies as decided upon in team meetings and/or as directed by case specialist or supervisor
  • Will participate in the Registered Behavior Technician (RBT) series (i.e., 40 Hour Training, Workshops, and BCBA assessment)
  • Bachelor’s degree in psychology, sociology, or other related field
  • Compensation based on Education and Experience (Hourly Based)
Apply

Clinical Director 

Qualifications

  • Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) Completed
  • Five plus years of experience in the field of Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA)
  • Experience training and supervising Behavior Technicians (BTs)
  • Experience revising progress reports and Functional Behavioral Assessments (FBAs)
  • Experience conducting workshops with BTs and parents
  • Experience conducting quality assurance checks for clinical cases
  • Experience conducting Functional Analysis (FAs)
  • Experience using different skill assessments (e.g., VB-Mapp, AFLS)
  • Open to receiving feedback from the Regional Director or Executive Director
  • Reliable Transportation 
  • Compensation based on Education and Experience (Salary Based)
Apply

Case Manager 

Qualifications

  • Two plus years of experience in the field of Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA)
  • Possess a Bachelors or Masters in ABA/Special Education (Preferred)
  • Need to be enrolled in an ABA or BCBA program
  • Experience in supervising and overseeing clinical cases
  • Experience implementing Discrete Trial Training (DTT)
  • Experience training Behavior Technicians (BTs)
  • In the process of attaining BCBA hours to sit for the exam
  • Experience writing progress reports
  • Experience conducting parent training
  • Experience with collecting data using different methods (e.g., time-sampling, rate) 
  • Experience using errorless, error correction, and chaining procedures (e.g., backward, forward)
  • Open to receiving feedback from Clinical Director or BCBA supervising
  • Reliable Transportation
  • Compensation based on Education and Experience (Hourly or Salary Based)
